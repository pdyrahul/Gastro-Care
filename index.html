Gastroenterology is a branch of medicine which deals with the disorders of the digestive system. We at GastroCare are a team of dedicated and experienced medical professionals who can provide best solutions for all your concerns relating to the digestive system

About Dr. Lokesh Nath Jha

+

Dr. Lokesh Nath Jha - Specialist in all types of liver and intestinal diseases with special interest in Inflammatory bowel disease.

Dr. Jha is a proficient consultant gastroenterologist, who did his senior residency in department of gastroenterology at G.B. Pant Hospital during from 2015- 2018. He completed his MBBS and MD (Medicine) from Vardhman Mahavir Medical College and Safdarjung Hospital and is currently working as consultant in Gastroenterology Department of Mata Chanan Devi hospital in Janakpuri, New Delhi. With extensive experience in his hands, Dr. Lokesh Nath Jha has performed approximately more than 1000 upper GI endoscopies and colonoscopies till date.
